Air Quality and Public Health Analysis

This project explores how air quality impacts public health, with a particular focus on the prevalence of Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) in various counties across the United States. Utilizing data from EPA, CDC, BEA, and the US Census Bureau, we provide insights into the correlation between air pollution levels and health outcomes.
